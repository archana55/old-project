My Summer Vacation

This Summer, we went on a week long road trip. My parents joined us on this trip as well. It was a very fun trip.

Our first stop on this road trip was Niagara Falls. We drove from St. Louis to Niagara Falls via Columbus. In Niagara Falls, we stayed for 2 days and visited all the falls.

Our next step was New York City. We drove from Niagara Falls to New York. We stayed for 2 days and visited several landmarks. Also went on a sightseeing tour. we had a great time.

Our Final stop was Washington DC. It was a short drive from NYC to Washington DC. We saw all the famous landmarks in DC and took a lot of pictures.

It was a fun trip. I enjoyed it very much
